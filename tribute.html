About The Vishwas Nangare Patil


Vishwas Nangare Patil was born in Kokrud village in Battis Shirala
taluka of Sangli district on 5th October. Further, his father was the Sarpanch of the town.
Later, he completed his schooling in taluk school, got 88% in 10th std,
and For his 12th standard, he took the Science stream but later opted for Arts for graduation.
Then, completed BA in History at Shivaji University, Kolhapur.
Further, he completed MBA from Osmania University.
Besides, At the college hostel, Bollywood superstar R Madhvan was his roommate.
After his graduation, he started preparations for the IPS exam.
Further, Vishwas Nangare Patil’s wife is Rupali Nangre Patil.
His son’s name is Ranveer Nangare Patil, and his daughter’s name is Janhavi Nangare Patil. Further, on 26/11 terror attacks on Mumbai, IPS Officer Vishwas Nangare Patil has shown bravery. Mumbai police during this attack and their struggle to save the lives by risking their lives.
